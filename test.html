HOW TO PLAY

Control your shadow warrior using the buttons or keyboard keys:

A or PUNCH button: Quick attack (10 damage)

S or KICK button: Strong attack (20 damage)

D or SHADOW STRIKE button: Special attack (30 damage)

F or BLOCK button: Block enemy attacks (50% damage reduction)

Reduce your opponent's health to zero to win!
