About Our School

Excellence in Education Since 2015

Empowering Young Minds in Balrampur

Located near Baluha Police Chowki, Milli Karwaan Public School provides a nurturing environment where students grow academically, socially, and ethically.

Our curriculum blends CBSE guidelines with innovative teaching methods, extracurricular activities, and character-building programs to ensure well-rounded development.

We believe every child is unique, and our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to unlock their full potential.
