About Our School

Excellence in Education Since 2009

Milli Karwaan Public School is a premier educational institution in Balrampur, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and creative thinking.

Our school follows the CBSE curriculum with a focus on activity-based learning, digital classrooms, and experiential education that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.

We believe in creating an environment where every child feels valued, respected, and inspired to reach their full potential.
